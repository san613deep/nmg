FAQ’s.


How much does it cost to develop an eCommerce website?

The cost of developing an eCommerce website varies based on features,
complexity, and technology. Depending on your specific requirements, the
average cost can range from $5,000 to $50,000 & beyond.


What are eCommerce website development services?

eCommerce website development services encompass designing, developing,
testing, and deploying an online store that enables customers to buy and sell
products on the web. It also includes UI/UX design, programming, integration,
and customization.


What is the difference between eCommerce websites and traditional websites?

eCommerce websites are designed to facilitate online transactions and offer
diverse products or services for purchase, while traditional websites typically
focus on providing information about the brand and its offerings. They do not
have direct e-commerce capabilities to let customers buy products online.


How long does it take to develop an eCommerce website?

The development time for an eCommerce website ranges from a few weeks to
several months, depending on the project's complexity, the technology utilised,
and the scale of the project. The timeline is established only after gaining a
proper understanding of project requirements.


How do you ensure the security of an eCommerce website?

You must hire eCommerce developers who ensure your site is secure by
implementing robust security measures such as SSL encryption, firewalls etc.
Regular site monitoring and security audits are conducted to ensure the same at
all times.
