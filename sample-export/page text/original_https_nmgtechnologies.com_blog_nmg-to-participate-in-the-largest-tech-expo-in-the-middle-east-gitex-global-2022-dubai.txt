GITEX Global 2022 is all set to take place in Dubai with its huge showcase of
enterprise solutions between 10-14 October 2022.

Learn about the latest happenings in the tech world, such as 5G, Artificial
Intelligence, the Metaverse, Cloud & Edge, Cybersecurity, Fintech, Blockchain,
Data Analytics & Smart Cities, and much more. GITEX Global 2022 invites over
4000+ exhibitors, 800+ startups, and 1000+ speakers from 170+ countries.

This year, Award-Winning Mobile App & Software Development Company NMG
Technologies will also participate in the GITEX Global 2022. Incepted in 2008,
NMG is one of the most reputed firms offering Mobile Apps, Custom Web/ Software
Development (PHP, Python, .NET, JAVA), AI/ ML applications, Ecommerce, Testing
and Search Marketing services. With offices across the USA and India, we focus
on growth opportunities in these respective markets.

With its exceptional services, NMG has worked with NASDAQ Listed Enterprise
clients to Tech startups based in the US. In addition, we have serviced some
clients in the United Arab Emirates, Oman, Kuwait, and Bahrain and are now
expanding our business into the Middle East. Also, we operate in various
business verticals and have served some top Blue Chip clients, including RGP,
Nikon, WHO, Oxfam, HelpAge, etc.

Rohit Maheshwari, Co-founder & Director, NMG Technologies, commented: “We’re
delighted to be participating in GITEX Global as we usher in a new era for
software development technology. Our association with GITEX Global 2022 means
so much to us. We believe that transferring knowledge should be an important
part of this effort because it will help complete the tech transformation
journey.”

The NMG Technologies’ team will be at stand SR-F40, Booth 006, Sheikh Rashid
Hall, India Pavilion, as the company has a strong reputation for collaborating
and bringing innovative technologies to the people. Anyone can come to us and
present their ideas during the event. One can also book an appointment or email
us at gitex2022@nmgtechnologies.com .

About NMG Technologies

NMG is a global software company that provides custom development services. We
have served more than 500 clients by offering services such as Mobile app
development, Web development, Software development, Python development, Beta
Testing, Automation Testing, E-Commerce development, and many others. We use
the latest technologies to ensure quality during the process. NMG Technologies
takes pride in its work with 14 years of experience and global clients!
