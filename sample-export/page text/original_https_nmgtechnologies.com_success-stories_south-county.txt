PROJECT OVERVIEW

South County Hospital stands out as the sole institution in Rhode Island and
the broader Northeast region to consistently earn 5-Star ratings from the
Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) for both Hospital Quality and
Patient Experience. This prestigious recognition underscores the hospital's
dedication to excellence in patient care and service.

Complementing this achievement is the establishment of the South County Medical
Group, a fully integrated network of specialty and primary care providers. This
collective is strategically designed to deliver a seamless continuum of care to
patients, ensuring a cohesive healthcare experience that addresses a broad
spectrum of medical needs.

A pivotal advancement that marks a significant leap in healthcare accessibility
and patient engagement is the launch of a mobile application dedicated to South
County Health Services. This innovative app is meticulously crafted to
prioritize user convenience, boasting a user-friendly interface that guides
individuals through various healthcare segments with ease. It simplifies the
process of accessing comprehensive information on the health system’s services,
from specialist care to primary healthcare provisions.

The application essentially serves as a digital portal to South County Health
Services, offering an efficient means for users to stay abreast of the health
resources, services, and facilities at their disposal. Its carefully designed
interface facilitates smooth navigation across different healthcare categories,
allowing users to quickly find detailed information on medical specialties,
available facilities, programs, and much more.

By integrating technology with healthcare, South County has effectively
enhanced the way health information and services are delivered, making it more
convenient for users to engage with and benefit from the extensive range of
healthcare solutions it offers.

contribute to and benefit from the hospital's mission. Here, we outline several
key areas of engagement:


Make a Donation

They immediately and positively impact the delivery of services to patients by
supporting future technologies, building modern infrastructures, and developing
various health programs needed in the community.


Volunteer

There are many volunteer patients who contribute their time and participation
to additionally support patient care and contribute to daily hospital
experiences.


Planned Giving

One way to create a strong last will is to give bequests and charitable trusts,
which will assist in the sustainability of South County Health Services.


Find a Doctor

With our extensive list of available doctors, you can easily find the
appropriate primary care physicians and specialists in our network to provide
you with the necessary individualized and integrated approach to your health
condition.


Career Opportunities

Patients & Visitors

FEATURES

South County Health Services stands out in the healthcare industry with its
innovative approach and comprehensive offerings. The institution’s framework is
designed to seamlessly integrate various healthcare components, creating an
environment where patient care and technological advancement go hand in hand.
From providing top-tier specialist and primary care through its South County
Medical Group to pioneering patient engagement with its mobile application,
South County Health Services embodies the pinnacle of modern healthcare. The
app’s intuitive interface enhances user experience and ensures easy access to a
wide range of health services and detailed information, significantly
simplifying the healthcare journey for patients.

South County Health Services is not limited to basic healthcare provision; it
embraces social responsibility and promotes community participation in several
ways. Some common ways people can get involved are by giving donations,
volunteering, or engaging in planned giving programs. These are important in
improving patient care, funding new technology, and sustaining community health
programs. Furthermore, South County provides a comprehensive directory to
assist users in finding doctors and exploring career opportunities within its
nurturing work environment. This inclusive approach ensures that whether you
are a patient, a potential employee, or a community member, your engagement
with South County Health Services translates to reaping the benefits of a
holistic and forward-thinking healthcare system.
