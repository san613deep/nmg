TECH


Technologies we use for Shopify Development.

    • Core Technologies
    • Front-End Technologies
    • Back-End Technologies
    • Ruby on Rails
      This is the backbone of the Shopify platform, handling the server-side
      logic and database interactions.
    • Liquid
      Shopify's templating language, used to create the front-end structure and
      display dynamic content.
    • JavaScript
      Used for front-end interactivity, dynamic updates, and enhancing user
      experience.
    • HTML, CSS
      Standard web technologies for structuring and styling the front-end of
      Shopify stores.
    • React
      Shopify has increasingly adopted React for building interactive and
      dynamic user interfaces, particularly in the Shopify admin and for
      creating custom storefronts with Hydrogen.
    • TypeScript
      This is a superset of JavaScript that adds static typing, improving code
      maintainability and reducing errors. Shopify uses TypeScript in
      conjunction with React.
    • Hydrogen
      Shopify's React-based framework for building custom storefronts with a
      focus on performance and developer experience.
    • MySQL
      A relational database used for storing and managing data.
    • Redis
      An in-memory data store used for caching and improving performance.
    • Memcached
      Another caching system used to speed up data retrieval.
    • Kubernetes, Docker
      Containerization technologies used for deploying and managing
      applications at scale.

Our Development Process


Our Shopify Development Process.

    • 01
      Brainstorming and Planning

      Our Shopify development process begins with a comprehensive discovery
      phase. We'll start with a detailed questionnaire and consultation to
      understand your business goals, target audience, brand identity, product
      catalog, and specific requirements for your online store. This includes
      discussing desired functionalities, integrations, design preferences, and
      budget considerations. Based on this information, we'll create a project
      scope document outlining the project's objectives, deliverables, and
      timeline.
    • 02
      Design and Prototyping
      Once the planning phase is complete, our design team will create mockups
      and prototypes of your Shopify store. This includes designing the overall
      layout, homepage design, product page templates, navigation structure,
      and other key elements of the user interface. We'll present these designs
      to you for feedback and revisions. This iterative process ensures that
      the final design aligns perfectly with your vision and brand aesthetic.
      We can work with existing brand guidelines or develop a new visual
      identity for your online store.
    • 03
      Development and Configuration
      With approved designs in hand, our Shopify developers will begin the
      development process. This involves setting up your Shopify store,
      configuring settings, customizing a theme (or developing a custom theme),
      integrating necessary apps, and implementing the agreed-upon
      functionalities. This includes setting up product catalogs, collections,
      payment gateways, shipping options, and other essential e-commerce
      features. We focus on clean code, performance optimization, and adherence
      to Shopify best practices.
    • 04
      Testing and Quality Assurance
      IBefore launch, we conduct thorough testing and quality assurance to
      ensure your Shopify store functions flawlessly across different devices
      and browsers. This includes testing website performance, user experience,
      functionality, security, and responsiveness. We'll address any bugs or
      issues identified during testing and ensure the store meets our
      high-quality standards. This stage includes cross-browser and
      cross-device testing, user acceptance testing (UAT), and performance
      testing.
    • 05
      Launch and Deployment
      Once testing is complete and you've given final approval, we'll launch
      your Shopify store. This involves transferring the store to your chosen
      domain, configuring DNS settings, and ensuring everything is live and
      accessible to the public. We'll also provide training and documentation
      on how to manage your Shopify store, including adding products,
      processing orders, and managing content. We will assist you with setting
      up necessary accounts and configurations for a smooth launch.
    • 06
      Maintenance and Support
      Our commitment doesn't end with the launch. We offer ongoing maintenance
      and support services to ensure your Shopify store continues to run
      smoothly. This includes regular backups, security updates, performance
      monitoring, bug fixes, and technical support. We can also provide ongoing
      optimization, marketing support, and further development services to help
      you grow your online business. This ensures your store remains secure,
      up-to-date, and performs optimally.
