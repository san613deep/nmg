NMG Technologies to Participate in Retail Innovation Conference and Expo 2023
in Chicago

Chicago, IL - May 30, 2023 - NMG Technologies, a leading provider of advanced
SEO and web development services, is excited to announce its participation in
the upcoming Retail Innovation Conference and Expo, scheduled to take place
from June 13 to June 15, 2023, at McCormick Place in Chicago.

The Retail Innovation Conference and Expo is a premier gathering of industry
professionals, digital experts, and retail enthusiasts. With a focus on
ecommerce, the event provides a platform for attendees to explore the latest
trends, advancements, and innovative solutions in online retail. Panel
discussions, keynote presentations, and networking opportunities abound,
fostering knowledge exchange and showcasing cutting-edge technologies.

As a prominent participant in this esteemed event, NMG Technologies will
exhibit its comprehensive suite of ecommerce solutions. With a deep
understanding of the e-commerce landscape, NMG Technologies specializes in
providing advanced services such as ecommerce website development, mobile app
development, and digital marketing strategies. Leveraging their expertise in
search engine optimization (SEO), user interface and user experience (UI/UX)
design, pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, content marketing, and more, NMG
Technologies empowers businesses to drive organic traffic and enhance their
online presence.

Commenting on the event, Rohit Maheshwari, Partner at NMG Technologies,
emphasized the significance of the Retail Innovation Conference and Expo,
stating, "Participating in this event allows us to connect with industry
leaders and demonstrate our expertise in advanced SEO and web development. We
are thrilled to have the opportunity to share our knowledge and insights while
exploring new avenues for customer acquisition and brand growth."

NMG Technologies has a rich history of participating in industry conferences
and events. For example, in 2019, Partner Mohit Maheshwari delivered an
impactful speech on "Merchandising around out of stock to save the sale." With
a deep understanding of the ecommerce landscape, NMG Technologies has
collaborated with renowned brands, delivering exceptional results and driving
success across various industries.

To learn more about NMG Technologies and its comprehensive e-commerce services,
please visit the official website at https://nmgtechnologies.com/


About NMG Technologies

NMG Technologies is a leading provider of comprehensive e-commerce solutions,
specializing in website and mobile app development, digital marketing, and
UI/UX design. With a team of 150 professionals, NMG Technologies has a proven
track record of driving success for e-commerce businesses. Their advanced SEO
strategies, PPC advertising, content marketing, and digital PR services have
helped numerous clients achieve their organic traffic goals and enhance their
online presence. NMG Technologies is committed to delivering advanced
e-commerce solutions that enable businesses to thrive in the competitive online
retail landscape.

NMG Technologies has worked with top brands such as Bacardi, Nikon, Intuit,
Harvard Alumni Network, Avery Dennison, London and Partners, World Health
Organization, and Oxfam. Additionally, they have collaborated with e-commerce
stores, startups, and SMBs in over 30 countries. Certified by Google and
SEMrush Agency Partners, NMG Technologies has significantly impacted various
industries.
