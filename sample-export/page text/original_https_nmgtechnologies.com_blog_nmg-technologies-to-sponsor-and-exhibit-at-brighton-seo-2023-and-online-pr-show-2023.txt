NMG Technologies to Sponsor and Exhibit at Brighton SEO 2023 and Online PR Show
2023

Discover the latest news and updates from NMG Technologies as we announce our
sponsorship and exhibition at Brighton SEO 2023 and Online PR Show 2023.

Nishtha
Mar 29, 2023
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Estimated reading time: 13 minutes


NMG Technologies will showcase its Advanced SEO and Web development services in
front of 3500 senior Digital Marketing industry professionals at Brighton
Centre, Brighton, UK

NMG Technologies, a full-service digital marketing services provider, is
delighted to participate in the highly anticipated Brighton SEO 2023 event,
scheduled for April 20-21, 2023, and the Online PR show ( a Brighton SEO fringe
event) on 19th April 2023. This event is one of the most significant gatherings
in the UK digital marketing industry, attracting professionals, experts, and
enthusiasts from around the UK, Europe, and internationally to exchange
knowledge, build connections, and exhibit the latest trends and strategies in
the SEO industry.

Brighton SEO is a three-day conference from 19th April – 21st April 2023 that
brings together industry experts, innovators, and thought leaders to discuss
the latest trends and advancements in the digital marketing field. Attendees
can participate in workshops, panel discussions, and keynote presentations on
SEO, PPC, social media, content marketing, and more. NMG Technologies is
sponsoring this year’s event. NMG is also exhibiting at the Online PR show
2023, a BrightonSEO fringe event on 19th April 2023, and at the main
BrightonSEO 2023 from 20th -21st April 2023.

Mohit Maheshwari, Co-Founder of NMG Technologies, emphasized the significance
of this event, stating, “Brighton SEO offers an outstanding opportunity to
connect with other industry leaders, showcase our exceptional Advanced SEO
processes, E-commerce solutions using Magento, Shopify, Website development and
Mobile app development services as well as exchange knowledge and insights with
other attendees.”

Rohit Maheshwari, Co-Founder of NMG Technologies, expressed his excitement
about the event, stating, “We are privileged to be part of such a prestigious
event considering NMG started from the UK in 2008. I have great memories of
starting the company from my apartment in Barbican, and in our earlier years,
we focussed extensively on the UK market. Since then, NMG has grown across the
US, with sales offices in Los Angeles and Nigeria. So we are happy to be back
in the UK and sponsoring and exhibiting at BrightonSEO 2023.”

Mr. Kelvin Newman, Founder, Brighton SEO, also expressed his delight with NMG
Technologies joining this year’s event, stating, “It’s a great opportunity for
our attendees to benefit from NMG Technologies innovative Advanced SEO services
and Web development solutions at Brighton SEO 2023.”

About NMG Technologies

NMG is a digital marketing agency specializing in website and e-commerce
development, focusing on driving organic traffic through advanced SEO, link
building, and content marketing. Since 2008, they’ve offered various services,
including web development, UI/UX design, advanced SEO, PPC, CRO, link building,
and digital PR. Their team of 150 professionals has worked in SEO since 2000,
and they’ve helped clients achieve organic traffic goals through effective
strategies and processes.

NMG has worked with top brands Bacardi, Nikon, Intuit, Harvard Alumni Network,
Avery Dennison, London and Partners, World Health Organization, and Oxfam. In
addition, the company has collaborated with e-commerce stores, startups, and
SMBs in 30+ countries. They’re certified by Google and SEMrush Agency Partners,
and they’ve significantly impacted various industries. Their engagement models
range from fixed monthly costs to dedicated team setups based on time and
material basis.

NMG Technologies is also running a free Brighton SEO ticket giveaway for
clients and prospects filling in their project details on the page and
scheduling a meeting with NMG Team during BrightonSEO 2023

https://growth.nmgtechnologies.com/brightonseo.
