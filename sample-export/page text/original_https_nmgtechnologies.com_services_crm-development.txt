Our Development Process


Our CRM Development Process.

    • 01
      Brainstorming and Planning
      The CRM development process begins with a comprehensive discovery phase.
      We initiate this with detailed consultations and workshops with your team
      to understand your specific business needs, workflows, pain points, and
      goals for the CRM system. This includes identifying key stakeholders,
      defining user roles and permissions, outlining desired functionalities
      (e.g., sales automation, marketing campaigns, customer service tracking),
      and establishing key performance indicators (KPIs) for measuring success.
      This information is then used to create a detailed project scope document
      and a high-level project plan, including timelines and budget estimations.
    • 02
      CRM Design and Prototyping
      Based on the gathered requirements, our design team develops wireframes
      and mockups to visualize the user interface and user experience (UI/UX)
      of the CRM system. These prototypes focus on usability, intuitiveness,
      and alignment with your branding. We present these designs to you for
      feedback and iterate based on your input. This stage may also involve
      creating a clickable prototype to simulate the CRM's navigation and key
      functionalities, allowing for early user testing and validation.
    • 03
      CRM Development and Configuration
      Once the design is finalized, our development team begins building the
      CRM system. This may involve customizing an existing CRM platform (like
      Salesforce, Zoho, or HubSpot) or developing a custom CRM solution from
      scratch, depending on your needs and budget. This stage includes database
      design, custom coding, API integrations with other business systems
      (e.g., accounting, ERP), and configuration of workflows, automation
      rules, and reporting dashboards. Regular development updates and demos
      are provided to keep you informed of progress.
    • 04
      Testing and Quality Assurance
      Rigorous testing is conducted throughout the development process and
      after each major milestone. This includes unit testing, integration
      testing, system testing, and user acceptance testing (UAT). Our QA team
      tests the CRM for functionality, performance, security, and usability. We
      also work with your team to conduct UAT to ensure the CRM meets your
      business requirements and works seamlessly within your existing
      workflows. Bug fixes and necessary adjustments are made based on the
      testing results.
    • 05
      Deployment and Training
      After successful testing, the CRM system is deployed to a production
      environment. This involves migrating data from your existing systems,
      configuring server infrastructure, and ensuring system stability. We
      provide comprehensive training to your team on how to use the new CRM
      system effectively. This may include on-site training, online tutorials,
      and user manuals. We also offer ongoing support to address any questions
      or issues that may arise after deployment.
    • 06
      Ongoing Support and Maintenance
      Our commitment extends beyond the initial launch. We offer ongoing
      support and maintenance services to ensure the CRM system continues to
      perform optimally. This includes bug fixes, security updates, performance
      monitoring, and technical support. We also offer optional enhancements
      and upgrades to add new features or adapt the CRM to evolving business
      needs. Regular communication and feedback sessions are conducted to
      ensure your satisfaction and identify areas for improvement.
