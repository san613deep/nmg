WHAT WE DO

Founded in London by Rohit and expanded with a US presence by Mohit, NMG is an
award-winning digital solutions group with 18+ years of experience. We empower
businesses, from startups to NASDAQ-listed companies, through specialized
solutions in Software Development (AI/ML), Mobility, E-commerce, and Digital
Marketing.

With a global presence and a dedicated team of 150+ experts, we are your
partners in digital transformation, driving measurable growth and impactful
customer interactions. Operating from our state-of-the-art delivery centers in
Gurgaon, India, and Los Angeles, United States, we're committed to delivering
comprehensive support to help you thrive.

Our data-driven approach has helped clients achieve measurable growth, from MVP
validation to securing funding from top investors like Google, A16Z, Dreamit
Ventures, Bunat Ventures, and the like. As your dedicated partners, we are as
invested in your success as you are.
