Never Miss a Goal with Koora Break!

Developed by NMG Technologies, Experience Football Like Never Before with Saudi
Arabia's Award-Winning Soccer App.

NMG
Feb 23, 2024
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


NMG Technologies is thrilled to announce Koora Break - our newest app designed
for passionate football fans. It was recently recognized as one of Saudi
Arabia's Top 5 Best Soccer Apps for its exceptional user experience and
top-quality content.

Focused on providing real-time updates on matches involving your favorite
football clubs, this innovative app offers a comprehensive range of features
designed to cater to the needs of every football fanatic.

With its Match Previews and Reviews feature, users can dive deeper into the
world of football analysis, including in-depth previews of upcoming matches,
complete with expert predictions. After the game, they can access comprehensive
reviews, highlights, and key performances.

Since its launch in 2022, Koora Break has grown to attract 800 million monthly
views.
UK football star Rio Ferdinand’s The Ferdinand Group plans to invest in this
Saudi Arabia-based sports platform - Koora Break, to help it expand across
Europe and Asia. “Koora Break is a great example of how the sports and tech
ecosystems are producing homegrown champions,” Ferdinand said in the statement.

Koora Break is just one example of our commitment to developing
high-performing, engaging applications. Partner with NMG Technologies to create
the next winning sports app.
