FAQ’s.


What is Magento in programming?

Magento is a PHP-based, open-source e-commerce platform that helps merchants
build and manage online stores with ease.


What language is Magento written in?

Magento is written in PHP. Its frontend layer consists of HTML, CSS, and
JavaScript and its database layer uses MySQL or PostgreSQL.


What are the benefits of Magento custom extension development?

The benefits of Magento custom extension development include increased
flexibility, scalability and customization. This allows businesses to tailor
online storefronts to their specific needs and offer a better UX.


What is Magento 2 custom module development?

Magento 2 custom module development is the process of creating custom
extensions and modules for the Magento 2 e-commerce platform. This allows
developers to extend the platform’s functionalities, fix bugs, and improve
performance.


How long does it take to develop an e-store using Magento?

The time it takes to develop an e-store using Magento depends on the project’s
complexity, the scope of work and the e-store’s size. On average, it can take
several weeks to several months.


What would be the average cost of developing a Magento ecommerce store?

The average cost of developing a Magento ecommerce store, factoring in hosting,
domain registration and an SSL certificate can range from $10,000 to $50,000.
The cost can be higher depending on the complexity of the project.


Is Magento a good ecommerce platform?

Yes, Magento is a widely-used ecommerce platform, offering an array of
features, flexibility and scalability that makes it suitable for businesses of
all sizes.
